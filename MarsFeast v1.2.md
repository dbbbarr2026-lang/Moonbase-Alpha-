### THE FIVE SYMBIOTIC LEGS (Fully Aligned with Challenge Lifecycle)

**Leg 1 – Greenhouse & Life Support (Production, Processing, Preparation, Storage)**  
Expanded shielded volume from tipped/buried Starships hosts hydroponics/aeroponics + soil-based greenhouses in bioremediated regolith (perchlorates reduced to chloride + O₂). Crops: diverse staples (wheat/barley for sourdough flatbread, potatoes, leafy greens, tomatoes, peas, etc.) + algae/microbes for supplements. Methane-fueled griddles/stovetops (ISRU or Raptor-powered/heat) enable fresh preparation. Storage in pressurized hull sections. Output: 100% nutritional base + ritual feasts.  

**Leg 2 – Waste Recapture & Resource Regeneration**  
Human/plant/cattle waste digested in repurposed Starship tanks → biogas (methane for energy/cooking) + nutrient-rich slurry for Leg 1/3. Cattle manure enhances loops. Minimizes waste mass; recycles water/nutrients at high efficiency.  

**Leg 3 – Regeneration, Terraforming Seeds & Shielding**  
**Core**: Tipped-and-buried Starships as primary initial shielded habitats/greenhouses. Process (first-principles feasible in 0.38g): drain tanks, controlled tip (Optimus/cables/counterweights/regolith piling; reduced weight lowers risk), trench placement, 5–10+ m regolith/ice berms (radiation attenuation to safe levels; thermal mass; meteoroid protection; overburden pressure scaled by 0.38g remains low). Internals converted using baffles for floors/walls; Prufrock tunnels connect modules. Bioremediation (PRB microbes like *Azospira oryzae*) treats surrounding/batch regolith before/during burial. Yields rapid, large-volume growing space + protection.  

**Leg 4 – Power & Methalox Production (Resilience Engine)**  
**Core**: Repurposed Raptors (5 per Starship at ~50% throttle for longevity/stress reduction) in shielded pods or integrated with hulls. Fuel: local ISRU methalox (unlimited from atmosphere/ice). Provides baseload/heat during solar outages; hybrid with dust-mitigated solar + Optimus cleaning. Exhaust (H₂O + CO₂) recyclable or vented safely. Synergizes perfectly with all legs (powers bioreactors, lighting, processing, Optimus; heat aids digestion/greenhouses). Enables continuous food production.
